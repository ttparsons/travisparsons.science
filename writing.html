2017

The repertoire of epithelial morphogenesis on display: Progressive elaboration of Drosophila egg structure

Mechanisms of Development

This review paper was co-authored by myself and another member of the lab along with our advisor, and highlights the 'repertoire' of morphologies and cell behaviors within the Drosophila ovary as egg chambers develop from a single ball of cells into a highly complex functional tissue. This paper was published in a special "Collective Cell Migration" issue of the journal in the summer of 2017.
